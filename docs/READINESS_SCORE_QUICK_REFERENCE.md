# Readiness Score Quick Reference Guide
## For Rachel's StageMate Property Assessments

---

## SCORING SYSTEM AT A GLANCE

**Total Score: 0–100 points** (Five Pillars × 20 points each)

| Grade | Score Range | Meaning | Seller Action |
|-------|-----------|---------|---------------|
| **A** | 90–100 | Excellent, move-in-ready | Minimal work, list now |
| **B** | 80–89 | Good, minor updates | Small fixes ($500–$1,500) |
| **C** | 70–79 | Average, some work needed | Moderate fixes ($1,500–$3,000) |
| **D** | 60–69 | Below average, significant investment | Major fixes ($3,000–$10,000) |
| **F** | <60 | Critical issues | Major structural work needed |

---

## THE FIVE PILLARS (20 points each)

### Pillar 1: Emotional Connection (20 points)
**Question:** Does the buyer feel welcomed + can envision their life here?

**Scoring guide:**
- 18–20: Warm, inviting, personalized (minimal owner items), move-in-ready feeling
- 14–17: Nice, but missing one element (maybe dated paint or minimal styling)
- 10–13: Functional but cold; feels like a "rental" or institutional
- 6–9: Significant depersonalization needed; too much owner identity visible
- 0–5: Completely uninviting; buyer wouldn't see themselves here

**Quick check:**
- ✅ Entry is welcoming? (+3 points)
- ✅ Rooms feel warm, not institutional? (+3 points)
- ✅ Personal items removed/minimal? (+3 points)
- ✅ Overall "home" feeling? (+3 points)
- ✅ Décor is neutral + tasteful? (+2 points)
- ✅ Clutter-free surfaces? (+3 points)

---

### Pillar 2: Functionality & Flow (20 points)
**Question:** Does the home work well? Are spaces accessible + livable?

**Scoring guide:**
- 18–20: Excellent flow; furniture floated; clear pathways; rooms serve purpose
- 14–17: Good flow; mostly floated furniture; some clutter
- 10–13: Adequate layout; some furniture against walls; moderate clutter
- 6–9: Poor flow; cramped feeling; cords/clutter visible
- 0–5: Dysfunctional layout; rooms feel blocked; unusable spaces

**Quick check:**
- ✅ Furniture floated (not against walls)? (+3 points)
- ✅ Clear traffic flow + pathways? (+3 points)
- ✅ Rooms serve their intended purpose? (+3 points)
- ✅ Cords/wires hidden? (+2 points)
- ✅ Minimal clutter on surfaces? (+3 points)
- ✅ Negative space visible (30–40% of rooms empty)? (+3 points)

---

### Pillar 3: Light & Brightness (20 points)
**Question:** Does the home feel bright, airy, and healthy?

**Scoring guide:**
- 18–20: Bright + airy; excellent natural light; warm-toned lighting; no dark corners
- 14–17: Good light; some dark areas; mostly warm-toned
- 10–13: Mixed lighting; some areas dark; some warm, some cool
- 6–9: Generally dark; heavy drapes; cool-toned lighting; feels gloomy
- 0–5: Very dark; windows blocked; poor/no lighting; damp feeling

**Quick check:**
- ✅ Natural light flowing freely? (+3 points)
- ✅ Windows clean + unobstructed? (+3 points)
- ✅ Warm-toned lighting (3000K–3500K)? (+3 points)
- ✅ No dark corners or gloomy rooms? (+3 points)
- ✅ Overall "healthy" airy feeling? (+3 points)
- ✅ Color palette is light/neutral? (+2 points)

---

### Pillar 4: Storage & Organization (20 points)
**Question:** Does the home feel spacious? Are storage solutions visible + organized?

**Scoring guide:**
- 18–20: Closets clearly organized; pantry spacious; abundant negative space
- 14–17: Good organization; some clutter; adequate storage perception
- 10–13: Moderate organization; neutral perception of storage
- 6–9: Overcrowded closets/storage; limited negative space
- 0–5: Chaotic organization; no visible storage; cramped feeling throughout

**Quick check:**
- ✅ Closets appear organized + spacious? (+3 points)
- ✅ Pantry/storage visible + accessible? (+3 points)
- ✅ Surfaces clutter-free? (+3 points)
- ✅ Negative space visible (30–40% empty)? (+3 points)
- ✅ Buyer perceives "room to move"? (+3 points)
- ✅ Matching hangers/organized items visible? (+2 points)

---

### Pillar 5: Condition & Maintenance (20 points)
**Question:** Does the home feel well-maintained + trustworthy?

**Scoring guide:**
- 18–20: Spotless; paint fresh; fixtures all work; no damage/stains visible
- 14–17: Clean; minor scuffs; mostly functional; no major concerns
- 10–13: Generally clean; some scuffs/wear; minor issues
- 6–9: Dirty in places; scuffed surfaces; some broken fixtures
- 0–5: Dirty/grimy; stains visible; multiple broken items

**Quick check:**
- ✅ Surfaces clean + dirt-free? (+3 points)
- ✅ Paint fresh (no scuffs/stains)? (+3 points)
- ✅ Fixtures functional (doors, windows, lights)? (+3 points)
- ✅ No water stains/damage visible? (+3 points)
- ✅ Overall "cared for" feeling? (+3 points)
- ✅ No mold/musty smells? (+2 points)

## RED FLAG SCORING DEDUCTIONS

**Automatic deal-killers (apply in addition to pillar scores):**

| Red Flag | Deduction | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Water stains/mold | -10 pts | Deal-killer; must fix before listing |
| Roof damage | -10 pts | Major expense signal |
| Foundation cracks (major) | -10 pts | Structural concern |
| Pest evidence | -10 pts | Automatic rejection |
| Lingering odors | -8 pts | Trust killer |
| HVAC neglect | -3 pts | Maintenance signal |
| Poor plumbing | -3 pts | Expensive to fix |
| Electrical issues | -3 pts | Code violations |

## SCORING FORMULA

**Step 1:** Score each pillar (0–20 points)
- Pillar 1: Emotional Connection ___ / 20
- Pillar 2: Functionality & Flow ___ / 20
- Pillar 3: Light & Brightness ___ / 20
- Pillar 4: Storage & Organization ___ / 20
- Pillar 5: Condition & Maintenance ___ / 20

**Step 2:** Add pillars = Baseline score ___ / 100

**Step 3:** Deduct red flag points (if any) = **READINESS SCORE**

**Example:**
- Pillars: 16 + 14 + 15 + 13 + 12 = 70/100
- Red flags: -5 (HVAC neglect)
- **Final Readiness Score: 65/100 (D)**

## FIXES & ROI FRAMEWORK

For each of your Top 3 Fixes, include:

### Fix Structure Template

**1. Current Issue (describe what's wrong)**

**2. Why It Matters (buyer psychology impact)**
- How many points will fixing this add?
- Will it affect buyer perception (trust, functionality, appeal)?

**3. The Fix (specific action steps)**
- **Value Tier** ($20–150 per item): Budget-friendly options
- **Signature Tier** ($150–500 per item): Mid-range quality
- **Luxury Tier** ($500+ per item): Premium options

**4. Expected Cost (Signature tier)**
- Total investment: $XXX

**5. Expected Score Gain**
- Current score: 65/100
- Potential score: 75/100
- Gain: +10 points

**6. Payback Timeline**
- How quickly will this affect the sale?
- Days on market reduction?
- Sale price increase?
- Example: "This $500 fix = +$2,000–$5,000 sale price = 400% ROI"

**7. ROI Justification (for the seller)**
- "Invest $500, expect +$2,000–$5,000 in sale proceeds"
- "This fix reduces days on market by 7–14 days (worth $X/day)"

---

## QUICK ROI CALCULATION

**Formula:**
```
Investment: $[cost of fix]
Expected sale price increase: $[gain in proceeds]
ROI % = (Gain ÷ Investment) × 100

Example:
Investment: $500 (bedside lamps)
Expected gain: $2,500 (from 65→75 score)
ROI: ($2,500 ÷ $500) × 100 = 500% ROI
```

## TIMELINE RECOMMENDATIONS

**2-Week Sprint:**
- High-impact fixes only: cleaning, paint, curb appeal
- Budget: $500–$1,500
- Score gain: +5–10 points
- Use when: Emergency listing, quick sale needed

**4-Week Standard:**
- Mid-level fixes: above + furniture rearrangement, lighting
- Budget: $1,500–$3,000
- Score gain: +10–20 points
- Use when: Normal timeline (3–6 weeks to listing)

**6+ Week Premium:**
- Complete staging: above + major upgrades, virtual staging
- Budget: $3,000–$10,000+
- Score gain: +20–35 points
- Use when: Luxury property, competitive market, time available

## ASSESSMENT TIPS FOR RACHEL

**When scoring, ask yourself:**

1. **Emotional Connection:** Would *I* want to live here? Why or why not?
2. **Functionality:** Can I move through this space easily? Do rooms work?
3. **Light:** Does this feel healthy and bright, or dark and gloomy?
4. **Storage:** Do I feel like there's room for my stuff here?
5. **Condition:** Does the owner care for this place? Will I inherit problems?

**Red flags to watch for:**
- ❌ Any smell besides "fresh"
- ❌ Water stains (ceiling, walls, basement)
- ❌ Creaky floors, squeaky doors (settling?)
- ❌ Obvious clutter or disorganization
- ❌ Dark rooms or broken light fixtures
- ❌ Overstuffed closets (opposite of spacious)

**Budget tier decisions:**
- **Value:** Minimal, quick fixes (paint, hooks, cleaning)
- **Signature:** Standard quality, better ROI (good lamps, decent furniture)
- **Luxury:** Premium finishes (designer pieces, high-end upgrades)

## EXAMPLE ASSESSMENT (for reference)

**Property:** 1234 Main St, Anytown

**Five Pillars:**
- Emotional: 12/20 (functional but cold; too much personal décor)
- Functionality: 14/20 (decent layout; some clutter on surfaces)

- Storage: 10/20 (closets disorganized; pantry overcrowded)
- Condition: 13/20 (clean overall; minor scuffs; HVAC old)

**Baseline:** 60/100

**Red flags:** -3 (HVAC neglect)

**READINESS SCORE: 57/100 (F)**

**Top 3 Fixes:**
1. Deep clean + depersonalize (-$200) → +8 points
2. HVAC service + filter (-$300) → +3 points
3. Paint + lighting updates (-$800) → +12 points

**Projected:** 80/100 (B) after ~$1,300 investment

**ROI:** +$3,000–$5,000 expected sale increase (230–385% ROI)
